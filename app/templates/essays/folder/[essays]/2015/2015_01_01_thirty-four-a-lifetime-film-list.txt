Thirty-Four (a lifetime film list)
====================

<p2>Just as I finished my <A href="/thirty-four-a-lifetime-mix/">lifetime music mix</a>, it occurred to me that a lifetime film list would be a good idea. So here it is. There were some very tough choices (see the spreadsheet link below), so the same unable-to-choose-just-one caveat applies to several years below. One additional mention, as it's current to this year, but I'm pretty shocked by the almost complete exclusion of *A Most Wanted Man* in almost every best-of-2014 list I've seen. Was it just forgotten?

If this inspires you to create a lifetime film list of your own, I'd love to see it!</p2>
